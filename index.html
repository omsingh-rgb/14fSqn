#14F

This is 14F Northolt Squadron. We are a Royal Air Force Air Cadets squadron based out of RAF Northolt. 👋

The Royal Air Force Air Cadets is one of the largest youth organisations in the United Kingdom. We aim to provide training for 13-20 year olds that will prepare them for later life.

Please feel free to browse our website and contact us if you are interested in joining.

"14F (Northolt) Squadron is a truly unique unit, not just because of our location on an active RAF station, but because of the incredible people who make it what it is. Our cadets and volunteers come together to create an environment that’s dynamic, inclusive, and constantly striving for excellence. We’re proud to offer a full and varied Air Cadet experience, with opportunities that reflect the spirit and professionalism of the Royal Air Force. It’s a privilege to lead a team so passionate about helping young people thrive.”
Sqn Ldr Chris Da Camara, Officer Commanding
